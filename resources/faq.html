Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about LibreGet

General Questions

LibreGet is a free, open-source download manager designed to enhance your downloading experience across Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms. We provide a fast, secure, and user-friendly tool that maximizes download speeds and offers advanced management features.

Yes, LibreGet is 100% free and will always remain so. We are a community-driven project committed to providing high-quality software without hidden costs, ads, or premium versions. Our mission is to empower users with powerful download tools.

Technical Details

LibreGet supports Windows (7 and above), macOS (10.14 and later), and Linux distributions. We provide native installers and portable versions for each platform, ensuring a seamless experience across different operating systems.

LibreGet uses advanced multi-connection technology that can speed up downloads by up to 12x compared to standard browsers. We intelligently manage connections, select optimal servers, and provide adaptive download acceleration.

Security and Privacy

Absolutely. LibreGet includes real-time virus scanning, secure HTTPS downloads, and download integrity verification. As an open-source project, our code is transparent and regularly reviewed by our community for security vulnerabilities.

No. We are committed to user privacy. LibreGet does not collect personal data, track user activities, or include any form of telemetry. Our software is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Open Source & Funding

Yes, LibreGet is 100% open-source. Our entire codebase is publicly available on GitHub, allowing anyone to view, review, modify, and contribute to the project. We believe in transparency and community-driven development, which means our source code is fully accessible to everyone.

LibreGet is primarily sustained through community support, donations, and the passion of volunteer developers. We do not rely on:

  • Selling user data
  • Inserting advertisements
  • Creating paid premium versions
  • Charging for features
Our funding model is based on voluntary contributions from users who believe in our mission of providing free, open-source software.

Absolutely! We welcome contributions in various forms:

  • Code contributions via GitHub
  • Bug reporting and testing
  • Documentation improvements
  • Financial support through donations
  • Spreading the word about the project
Visit our GitHub repository to get started or check out our support page to learn more about how you can help.

Our long-term sustainability relies on:

  • Community-driven development
  • Volunteer contributions
  • Transparent donation model
  • Minimal operational costs
  • Commitment to keeping the software free
We prioritize the project's mission over monetization, ensuring that LibreGet remains a free, high-quality tool for everyone.
